National Talent Search Competition 2026

About the competition

How do I get information about the National Talent Search Drawing and Painting Scholarship Competition 2026?

Visit www.kcaindia.com and open the Competition section, or use the Register Your School button on the homepage. All details are provided there.

Who can fill out the registration form and who can participate?

Only School Principals, Art Teachers or School Authorities are authorized to fill the School Registration Form. Students from Nursery to Class 10 from schools registered with KCA are eligible to participate through their respective schools.

How can my child participate? (For parents)

Visit the For Parents section on the homepage and share your child's school name and correspondence address with KCA. We will send the competition brochure to that school, and once the school registers, your child can participate.
