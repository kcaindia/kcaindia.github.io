Our Story

A powerful supplement to traditional schooling

Kids' Cerebral Academy (KCA) represents an innovative educational approach led by Dr. Gurupreet Kaur Saluja, the child psychologist who founded the organization. The academy operates as a powerful supplement to traditional schooling by promoting self driven learning across multiple developmental areas.

KCA has maintained its national standing for over a decade and a half, demonstrating real effectiveness in student development. Its approach embraces academic learning alongside physical, cerebral, cognitive and emotional skill building, because a child's growth is never one dimensional.

Our flagship: the National Talent Search

KCA specializes in the National Talent Search Drawing and Painting Scholarship Competition, spanning Nursery through Class 10. KCA holds a unique distinction as the only academy offering scholarships in this national competition, a reputation built over more than fifteen years.

The mega contest engages lakhs of students across the nation, targeting children aged 3 to 15 and promoting excellence and confidence through talent showcase opportunities. The academy provides prizes, scholarships and consolation rewards nationally, and honours the participating schools and educators who make it possible.

Founder's vision: Dr. Gurupreet Kaur's work emphasizes the psychological aspects of child development, aiming to inspire students and prepare them for the challenges of the twenty first century.
