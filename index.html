How it works

Three simple steps to participate

Step 1 · Schools

Register your school

Fill the online registration form. KCA sends your school a brochure with competition guidelines, deadlines and procedures.

Step 2 · Parents

Connect your child's school

Share your child's school correspondence address with KCA so the school receives full competition details.

Step 3 · Students

Draw, paint and shine

Students compete in their age category for scholarships, medals and national recognition of their creativity.

About KCA

A powerful supplement to traditional schooling

Kids' Cerebral Academy has revolutionized the landscape of education with its unique concept of self driven learning. Over 16 years, KCA has earned a national reputation for nurturing the holistic growth of children.

  • Holistic development: academic, physical, cognitive and emotional
  • Built on research into the psychology of child growth
  • National platform for young artists from age 3 to 15
  • Honours the schools and educators behind every young achiever

For Parents

Welcome Parents!

To ensure your child receives information about Rising Colours 2026, provide the correspondence address of your child's school. Once the school is registered with KCA, they receive a full competition brochure — with guidelines, deadlines and registration procedures.

By encouraging your child to participate, you give them an opportunity to showcase their creativity, compete for scholarships and awards, and gain national recognition for their artistic achievements.
