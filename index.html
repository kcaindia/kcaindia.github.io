How it works

How to Join

Step 1 · Schools

School registers first

Only School Principals, Art Teachers or School Authorities are authorised to fill the School Registration Form. Once a school is enrolled, all its students from Nursery to Class 10 become eligible to participate.

Step 2 · Brochure

Brochure by mail or WhatsApp

After registration, the official brochure and participation form are shared with the school by mail or WhatsApp, with guidelines, schedules and procedures.

Step 3 · Students

Draw, paint and shine

Students compete in their age category for scholarships, medals and recognition of their creativity.

Step 4 · Recognition

National visibility & recognition

Top achievers are featured on kcaindia.com, leading newspapers and social media.

About KCA

India's First & Only Art Scholarship Competition for Nursery to Class 10

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Gurupreet Kaur, Child Psychologist and MD of KCA, on the philosophy of Self Education through Creative Expression. KCA is the first and only academy in India to conduct national level Drawing & Painting Scholarship Competitions for Nursery through Class 10.

  • Thousands of young artists participating nationwide
  • Scholarships, medals, mementos and national recognition
  • Inspires confidence, cognitive growth and artistic excellence
  • Honours principals, art teachers, coordinators and schools
G

Dr. Gurupreet Kaur

Founder & MD, KCA · Child Psychologist

"Discover the Artist in Every Child."

Behind every young artist is an educator who believed in them, which is why KCA proudly honours principals, art teachers, coordinators, administrators and schools.

For Parents

Welcome Parents!

To ensure your child receives information about Rising Colours 2026, provide the correspondence address of your child's school. Once the school is registered with KCA, they receive a full competition brochure, with guidelines, deadlines and registration procedures.

By encouraging your child to participate, you give them an opportunity to showcase their creativity, compete for scholarships and awards, and gain national recognition for their artistic achievements.
