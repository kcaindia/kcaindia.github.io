National Talent Search Drawing & Painting Scholarship Competition 2026

Rising Colours — the mega contest that has inspired thousands of young achievers across India. Open to students from Nursery to Class 10.

Prizes & Awards

What winners take home

TOP 3
🥇

National Award Certificates

Top three finalists in each category receive National Award Certificates with Gold, Silver and Bronze medals. 1st Prize includes a National Scholarship Award of Rs 3000, Gold Medal, Memento and Merit Certificate.

🎖️

Merit Certificates

4th to 10th place winners in each category receive merit certificates recognising their talent at the national level.

📜

E-Consolation Certificates

Thousands of e-consolation certificates are bestowed upon deserving students so every effort is celebrated.

🏫

School Acknowledgment

School administrators, principals and art teachers receive acknowledgment awards for encouraging participation.

Categories

7 categories, Nursery to Class 10

Seven age categories span Nursery through Class 10, so every child from age 3 to 15 competes fairly with peers of their own level. Category details arrive with the official brochure after school registration.

Who can register

Schools register first

Only School Principals, Art Teachers or School Authorities are authorized to fill the School Registration Form. Once a school is enrolled, all its students from Nursery to Class 10 become eligible.

What happens next

Brochure by mail or WhatsApp

After registration, the official brochure and participation form are shared with the school by mail or WhatsApp, with guidelines, deadlines and procedures.

Recognition

National visibility

Top achievers are featured on kcaindia.com, leading newspapers and social media. National renowned personalities inspire awardees at the award ceremony.
