How to join

Register your school

Who can register

Schools register first

Only School Principals, Art Teachers or School Authorities are authorized to fill the School Registration Form. Once a school is enrolled, all its students from Nursery to Class 10 become eligible.

What happens next

Brochure by mail or WhatsApp

After registration, the official brochure and participation form are shared with the school by mail or WhatsApp, with guidelines, deadlines and procedures.

Recognition

National visibility

Top achievers are featured on kcaindia.com, leading newspapers and social media. National renowned personalities inspire awardees at the award ceremony.
